About Me

My name is Stella, I am a photographer, who has been living in Atlanta, GA for 8 years. I have been a teacher for over 20 years. Also let me add I love doing different types of arts and crafts. Deciding to follow my passion I picked up my camera and started snapping away.

The passion of the art of photography started 15 years ago when our little family was stationed in Alaska. During that time just being surrounded by the Alaskan wilderness is one of the best and breath taking experiences of my life. Those awe like moments I believe will inspire anyone to pick up a camera.
